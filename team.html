Co-Founders

Amnon Bar-Tur

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Amnon Bar-Tur is a seasoned real estate investor and entrepreneur with a distinguished track record in the global property market. Together with his son Armon, he co-founded BaseCamp Student, a leading student housing platform that became a market leader in Northern Europe. Under their leadership, the company expanded rapidly and was acquired in 2022 for $1 billion by Xior, a Belgian real estate investment trust — creating the largest European student housing company.

In addition to BaseCamp, Amnon has been actively involved in real estate investment and development across multiple asset classes, including residential, hospitality, and commercial properties. His expertise spans acquisitions, asset management, and value-driven strategies in international markets.

Nir Yanushevsky

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Nir Yanushevsky is the CEO of Yanushevsky Group, one of Israel's leading construction firms with a legacy dating back to 1946. With over 20 years of experience and a GC license, Nir has led the development of over 1,000 residential units in Tel Aviv, specializing in high-density urban renewal and demolition-rebuild projects.

Beyond residential work, the Group has executed major projects for Israel's most strategic sectors, including military and government infrastructure, and for international organizations including General Electric, Siemens, and Oracle. Today, he directs SafeHarbor's investments into transformative U.S.-based real estate opportunities.
